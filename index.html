IELTS Preparation

Level: Intermediate to Advanced

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is the most popular English language test in the world. For those who wish to study, work or migrate to places where English is the native language, a high score in the IELTS exam is beneficial. With our IELTS course, we can help you succeed.
 

About It  

Business Communications

Level: Intermediate to Advanced

This is a course designed to improve business specific English and help those who are looking to pursue a career in, or study, business. This course includes team business planning, company visits, individual bi-weekly feedback and Cambridge Business Certification.
 

About It  

Part-time English

Level: Elementary to Advanced

If you can’t attend a full-time English course you can take part in one of the many part-time options taught at our London school. You can plan classes around your own schedule and meet other students.
 

About It  

Long-term English

Level: Elementary to Advanced

Our Long-term English courses are ideal for those wanting to experience UK culture while improving their English. By studying for at least 12 weeks, you will have more time to enjoy the many sights and attractions the UK has on offer. Of course, the more weeks you stay, the more opportunity there will be for enjoyment and language development.
 

About It  

Communication Skills / Travel Talk

Level: Elementary to Upper Intermediate

If you're comfortable with your written English but feel that you need classes especially to develop your communication skills, this course could be the perfect choice for you.
 

About It  

Maritime English

Level: Elementary to Advanced

With our short-term General English course you can improve your level of English at our schools in London, Eastbourne or Dublin. You can also combine your study with additional courses and exam preparation classes with our English Plus+ and English Unlimited programmes.
